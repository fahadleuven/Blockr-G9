## Use Case 6: Scrolling  
### Scope: Blockr programming game
### Level: User goal
### Primary Actor: User of the game
### Stakeholders and Interests:
- Player: Wants to drag the slider to make another part
 of the contents visible.

### Preconditions:
- A vertical scrollbar shall be visible.
- The contents of an area not fit inside the area.
- The scrollbar consists of a slider and empty space above and below it.

### Success Guarantee (or Postconditions):
Blockr programming game window(when player draged the slider ) corresponds to 
the position of the visible part of the contents .

### Main Success Scenario;
1. User moves the mouse cursor over the scrollbar in the right side,
then presses the left mouse key on that side.

2. System makes a corresponding position of the visible part of the contents.
3. System shows the new window corresponding to 
the position of the visible part of the contents. 
 
 

### Extensions (or Alternative Flows):
*a At any time, user drag the slider with the left mouse key inside the area 
   of scrollbar.
	1. System shows the part of the contents that
 corresponding to the position of the slider.

1a. When the user click above the slider.
	1. System shows the part of the contents closer to 
the top of the contents.
	
1b. the User click below the slider.
	1. The system shows the part of the contents closer 
to the bottom of the contents.

*b. User click the left mouse key on an area that is out of area of scrollbar.
	1. The system donot change anythings.
	2. The system shows the part with current postion corresponding 
to the position of the visible part of the contents.

 *c the contents of an area fit inside the area.
      1.The system makes the scrollvar disable.
      2.The system shows Palette, the Program Area,
 and the Game World with origin position without scrolling.
